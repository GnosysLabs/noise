Why Noise

The internet made everything discoverable. We made belonging intentional again.

Noise is not a feed and your group is not content. There are no recommendations, follower counts, or strangers wandering in from search.

Create a group, share its 12-digit frequency, and let it travel the old-fashioned way: person to person.

Windows alpha

Windows may show a SmartScreen warning.

Noise is new, so its installer may not have enough download reputation yet. SmartScreen can warn about an unrecognized app even when it has not detected malware.

01
Open the downloaded installer.

If Windows says “Windows protected your PC,” stay on that screen.

02
Click “More info.”

Windows will reveal the filename and the option to continue.

03
Click “Run anyway.”

The normal Noise setup will begin.

Why this happens

SmartScreen uses reputation signals including download history and file signing. A warning does not automatically mean the file is malicious. Noise is open source, and every release is published from the public repository.
